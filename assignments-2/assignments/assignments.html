Assignments

There are 3 different types of activities. They are learning, try it out, and challenge activities.

The learning activities teach us new concepts and have us follow along in typing code to learn what these new concepts do and how we can use them.

The try it out activities give us a chance to practice what we the learning activiy we did taught us. We are supposed to make a website look the same as it does in the example by following along with the instructions.

The challenge activites are what we do last. We have one of them per module of learning and they are worth a lot of the grade. They combine what we learned throughout the module in the learning and try it out activities.
